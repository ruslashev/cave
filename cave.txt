CAVE engine by Ken Silverman      4/21/94        NOT FOR DISTRIBUTION.

	Welcome to the CAVE engine!  The editor is in the engine itself.
I like to hold down the space bar and move backwards to see the passage I
am making as it's being created.

	Editor:
Space Bar     = BLAST!  Clears a sphere out of the cave.
Ct.Space Bar  = S-U-P-E-R BLAST!  Clears a huge sphere out of the cave.
< / >         = Change blasting color (There's 4 colors, each with 64 shades)

	Movement:
Arrow / Mouse = movement
Rt. Ctrl      = strafe
Lt. Shift     = Double speed
A / Z         = Move up / down
+ / -         = Look up / down

	Screen control:
Q             = scan 320/320 columns (1*1 pixels)
W             = scan 160/320 columns (2*1 pixels)
E             = scan  80/320 columns (4*1 pixels)
R             = SMART detail (default) - if you're moving then uses mode W
													  if you're stopped then uses mode Q
S             = 320*400 resolution
D             = 320*200 resolution (default)
